Andrea Ajello

Assistant Director · Division of Monetary Affairs
Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System

I am an Assistant Director in the Division of Monetary Affairs at the Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System, overseeing the Macro-Financial Analysis section. My primary research fields are Macroeconomics, Financial Economics, and Applied Econometrics, with a focus on interest rate policy, financial stability, and inflation dynamics.
